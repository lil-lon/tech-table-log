## About Tech Table Log Podcast

"Tech Table Log" is a Japanese tech podcast hosted by Lon and Shinya.

### Hosts

- **Lon**: Software engineer / Machine learning engineer working in the AI4Science field
- **Shinya**: PostgreSQL engineer
- Both have approximately 6 years of industry experience
- Former colleagues who became friends and co-hosts

### Content Style

- **Tone**: Mix of casual conversation and technical discussion
- **Language**: Japanese only
- **Target audience**: Engineers in general (no specific domain expertise assumed)
- **Format**: No fixed structure; varies by topic

### Common Topics

- Database / Infrastructure (PostgreSQL, etc.)
- AI / Machine Learning (LLM, data science, etc.)
- Software development in general
- Random topics
